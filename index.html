Hello World!

My name is Arielle, and I am a native to the brilliantly sunny city of Los Angeles, CA. I spend most of my free time with friends and family, catching up on the latest TV series, baking novelty cupcakes, taste-testing LA's generous food scene, or learning how to code!

I became hooked; I not only enjoyed the work but was also eager to learn more and take a career in programming seriously. I am currently attending Dev Bootcamp and strive to take what I learn from this program and apply it to a career where the work I put in is creating a positive impact and helping others solve real problems.
